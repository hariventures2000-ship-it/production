HARI VENTURES — CORPORATE HR POLICIES & CODE OF CONDUCT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. LEAVE AND ATTENDANCE POLICY
   - Casual Leaves (CL): 12 days per calendar year. Credited at the start of the year. CL cannot be carried forward.
   - Sick Leaves (SL): 6 days per calendar year. SL requires a medical certificate if taken for more than 2 consecutive days.
   - Earned Leaves (EL): 15 days per calendar year, accrued at 1.25 days per month of service. A maximum of 30 EL days can be accumulated and carried forward to the next calendar year.
   - Maternity Leave: 26 weeks of fully paid leave for female employees.
   - Paternity Leave: 2 weeks of fully paid leave for male employees, to be taken within 6 months of child birth.
   - Sabbatical: Available for employees completing 5+ years of continuous service, up to 6 months unpaid.

2. WORK SCHEDULE & LOGISTICS
   - Standard Work Hours: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (IST).
   - Lunch Break: 1 hour, to be taken between 1:00 PM and 2:00 PM.
   - Remote Work (WFH): Up to 2 days per week, subject to approval by the direct reporting manager.
   - Core Hours: All employees must be online or present between 10:30 AM and 4:30 PM.

3. COMPENSATION & BENEFITS
   - Salary Cycle: Salaries are processed and credited directly to bank accounts on the last working day of every calendar month.
   - Reimbursements: Expense claims (travel, client entertainment) must be submitted by the 7th of the following month.
   - Health Insurance: Corporate health cover up to ₹5,00,000 (5 Lakhs) for the employee, spouse, and up to 2 dependent children.

4. OFFICE CODE OF CONDUCT
   - Dress Code: Smart Casuals (Monday to Thursday), Casual Fridays (Jeans and T-shirts allowed, no offensive graphics or slippers).
   - Notice Period: 60 days standard notice period for voluntary resignation by full-time regular employees.
   - IT Security: Do not share credentials, use MFA for all portal accesses, and do not connect personal drives to office laptops.
